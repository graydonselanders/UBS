Our History

Growing the collegiate climbing scene since 2016.

2016

The Beginning

The University Bouldering Series was founded to fill a gap in the Canadian climbing landscape. What started as a single competition between two local universities has grown into a multi-provincial series.

2019

Expansion

By 2019, the series expanded to include 5 distinct events across Ontario and Quebec, hosting over 400 unique student athletes.

Today

Varsity Aspirations

We continue to push for the recognition of climbing as a varsity sport, providing professional-grade competitions and community building for the next generation of climbers.
