Our Mission

Colleges and universities are incredible places to learn to climb. We are here to spread climbing in post-secondary education and bring students together.

Community First

Whether it's your first semester or you're a seasoned crusher, UBS strives for an encouraging atmosphere that represents the best of climbing culture.

Varsity Aspirations

Hopefully, if we get enough competitions going across Canada, we can make climbing a recognized varsity sport in the future.

Gym Partnerships

We assist gyms in putting on competitions. We have the connections and resources to help local gyms reach students effectively.
